About Me

Welcome to the world of data insights, where every piece of information holds the potential to transform businesses and drive success. I'm Ali Reza Arju, a Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst based in Toronto, Canada. With a wealth of experience spanning over 3+ years, I bring a diverse skill set and a passion for uncovering valuable insights from data.

In my journey as a Data Analyst, I've had the privilege of working in various capacities, from Operational Analyst at CIBC to Data Coordinator at Perflex Label Inc, and IT Officer at IFIC Bank PLC. Each role has equipped me with invaluable experience in extracting, analyzing, and visualizing data to drive informed decision-making processes.

My expertise lies in harnessing the power of tools like Microsoft Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau to derive actionable insights from complex datasets. Whether it's crafting detailed KPI reports or developing sustainable analytic dashboards, I thrive on the challenge of transforming raw data into meaningful information.

Beyond technical proficiency, I pride myself on possessing excellent organizational and communication skills, critical thinking abilities, and an unwavering attention to detail. As a multilingual individual fluent in English, Bengali, Hindi, and Japanese, I bring a global perspective to data analysis and interpretation.

With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from IUB, coupled with certifications such as DP-900 and PL 300, I am equipped to tackle the most demanding data challenges. Whether you're seeking insights to enhance business performance or looking to optimize operational efficiency, I'm here to help.

Thanks for exploring my portfolio and connect with me to unlock the full potential of your data. Let's embark on a journey of discovery, where data isn't just numbers but a catalyst for growth and innovation.

Welcome to the realm of data-driven decision-making. Let's make every data point count.
